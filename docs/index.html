Frequently Asked Questions

What is TapTrap?

TapTrap is a fast-paced reflex game. Tap the right tiles, avoid the wrong ones, and survive as long as you can. The rules change — stay sharp.

How do I restore my progress?

Sign in with your Apple ID or Google account to sync your stats and progress across devices. Guest progress is stored locally on your device.

How do I remove ads?

You can remove ads by upgrading to the ad-free experience from within the app. Go to Settings and look for the upgrade option.
