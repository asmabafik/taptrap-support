Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TapTrap ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application TapTrap: Don't Tap (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

About the App

TapTrap is a reflex-based mobile game. Players tap tiles as quickly and accurately as possible while avoiding traps. The game may include optional account features, leaderboards, and advertisements.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve the App:

  • Account information: If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and, optionally, your name and email address to create your account.
  • Game data: Your scores, stats, and game progress are stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, in our secure database.
  • Usage data: Anonymous crash reports and usage analytics may be collected to improve app stability and performance.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks, including Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You may be asked to consent to personalized advertising in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Tracking on iOS).

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, which may collect anonymous usage data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Sign in with Apple (authentication)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Expo / React Native (app framework and crash reporting)

Data Storage and Security

If you create an account, your data is stored securely. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Delete your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at asmabafik@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
